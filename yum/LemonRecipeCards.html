Lentils with Spinach and Lemon

serves 6 total time 95 min

  • lentils ½ pound
  • onions 1 cup sliced
  • olive oil ¼ of a cup
  • garlic 3 finely chopped
  • fresh coriander ¼ cup chopped
  • frozen spinach leaves 10 ounces
  • potatoes 2 sliced
  • lemon juice ¼ to taste
  • salt and pepper
Wash and pick over the lentils. Place lentils in a saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a boil. Cook, covered, about 20 minutes. Meanwhile, in a large casserole, brown the onions in oil. Stir in the garlic and coriander. Add the spinach and sauté 5 to 6 minutes, stir them frequently. Add the potatoes, lentils, and enough lentil cooking liquid to cover everything in the pot. Season with salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, lower the heat, and simmer for 1 hour or until it gets thick and soupy. Stir in the lemon juice. Serve hot, lukewarm or cold.
