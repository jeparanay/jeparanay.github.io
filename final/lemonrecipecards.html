When Life Gives You Lemons

I redesigned 10 lemon based recipes and also designed a packaging solution to hold them as a set. My packaging is designed to hold each recipe in a protective sleeve so it doesn't get messy as you cook in the kitchen. The recipies rotate around a centerpoint, which is removable to allow the user to hold the set of recipes as a booklet. The lemon base of the packaging also functions as a cutting board in the kitchen. The bottom of the board is equipt with rubber feet so it does not slide around on slippery counter surfaces. I handcut, painted, and assembled each element of my design.
