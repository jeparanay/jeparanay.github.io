Onomatopoeia Animation

After choosing an instrumental piece of music, I listened to the soundtrack and visually represented the song using words that correspond to each instruments sound. I displayed this part of my project in a typographic score, then made a flip book to show my idea for my animation. Finally, I created an animation which visually depicted my song choice.
